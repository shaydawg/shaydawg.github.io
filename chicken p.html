Ingredients Amount
Chicken breasts 4
Egg 1
Breadcrumbs 1 cup
Grated Parmesan cheese 1 cup
Olive oil 1/4 cup
Marinara sauce 2 cups
Steps
1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
2. Beat the egg in a shallow bowl.
3. Mix the breadcrumbs and Parmesan cheese in another shallow bowl.
4. Dip each chicken breast in the egg mixture, then coat in the breadcrumb mixture.
5. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the chicken and cook for 3-4 minutes on each side, until golden brown.
6. Transfer the chicken to a baking dish. Spoon marinara sauce over each chicken breast.
7. Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, until the chicken is cooked through and the sauce is bubbly.
8. Serve hot, garnished with additional grated Parmesan cheese and fresh basil leaves if desired.
